About me

Brian Koekkoek

Hi there, I'm Brian, currently living in Den Haag and as said, before I applied at Codaisseur, I was working in the offshore industry. What am I doing here you ask? Well, the offshore industry is on a massive downturn and you can't keep sitting 'at home' forever! When my rig got coldstacked, and I was made redundant after 13 years, I first decided to travel around the world. Visited countries like USA, Thailand, Spain, Greece, Malaysia and more. But after a great spell, reality kicked in, it was time to get back to business.

As I was travelling, and time was passing by, I started to explore other options outside of the offshore industry. Switch careers... It took me a long time, but in the end, I couldn't find an industry more booming and more interesting than the current technology industry, and web development really caught my eye!

So eventually I found Codaisseur and it really got me excited thinking about a different career path. Fast forward another few months and here I am, anxious to start!

In my free time I enjoy a lot of different hobbies, travelling, playing squash, both live and online poker, driving around on my motorbike and many others.
